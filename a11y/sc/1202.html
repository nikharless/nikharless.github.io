{{ currentpg.name }} (Level {{ currentpg.level | upcase }})

Success Criterion

Captions are provided for all prerecorded audio content in synchronized media, except when the media is a media alternative for text and is clearly labeled as such.

Definitions

captions
synchronized visual and/or text alternative for both speech and non-speech audio information needed to understand the media content
closed captions
can be turned on and off
media alternative for text
media that presents no more information than is already presented in text (directly or via text alternative)
open captions
cannot be turned off
subtitles
synchronized visual and/or text alternative for dialogue, often used for languages other than the audio language
synchronized media
audio or video synchronized with another format for presenting information and/or with time-based interactive components
