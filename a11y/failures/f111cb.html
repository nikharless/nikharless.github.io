WCAG | 1.1.1 Non-text Content | 1.1.1.c Complex Images (Text Alternative)

Expected Result

 The short text alternative for the complex image summarizes the information provided by the image.

Actual Result

 The complex image has a short text alternative, but it does not sufficiently summarize the information provided by the image.

Solution

Change the short text alternative to sufficiently summarize the information provided by the image.

Example

Complex Image with Link to Long Description

A short text alternative that reads "chart" would not sufficiently summarize the content of the image.

<figure role="group">
    <img src="constellations.png" alt="Historical chart of constellations." />
    <figcaption>
        <a href="long-description.html">Follow this link for a detailed description of the chart.</a>
    </figcaption>
</figure>

Why It's Important

Blind and visually impaired users need to have a short text alternative for complex images that summarizes its content, so that they can understand the purpose of the image and know whether they want to explore the long description.
