Overview

Basic Requirements and Best Practices

= Requirement
= Best Practice

  • Audio that is mostly speech must have low or no background sound or the background sound must be able to be turned off.
User Story: As a user who is hard of hearing, I want dialog to have low or no background sounds so that I can understand the dialog.
Success: If you were hard of hearing, when you listen to audio that is mostly speech, you would be able to understand the dialog.

Testing Logic

Does the page have prerecorded audio that is mostly speech?

  • Yes; Does the audio contain background sounds?
    • Yes; Is the background sound at least 20 decibels (4 times) quieter than the foreground speech?
      • Yes; PASS
      • No; Is there a mechanism to turn off the background sounds?
        • Yes; PASS
        • No; See {{ site.data.1407.1407[14070101].name }}.
    • No; PASS
  • No; This item is not applicable.

Test Procedure

Desktop

Audacity

  1. Open the audio file in Audacity.
  2. Select a section of the foreground audio.
  3. Select Analyze > Contrast....
  4. In the Contrast Analysis (WCAG 2 compliance) dialog, activate the Measure selection button in the Foreground row.
  5. Select a section of the background audio.
  6. In the Contrast Analysis (WCAG 2 compliance) dialog, activate the Measure selection button in the Background row.
  7. Verify that the result is 20 or greater.
