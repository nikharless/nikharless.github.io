Overview

Basic Requirements and Best Practices

= Requirement
= Best Practice

  • Regular text, including images of regular text, must have a contrast ratio of at least 7:1 with the background. The following are exceptions:
    • Text on disabled controls
    • Incidental text in images
    • Text in purely decorative images
    • Logos
User Story: As a user with a visual impairment, I want text to have good contrast so that I can more easily read it.
Success: If you had a visual impairment, when you viewed text, you would be able to see it well enough to read it.

Testing Logic

Does the page have text that is smaller than 18pt (24px) or 14pt (19px) bold?

  • Yes; Is the text background an image or gradient?
    • Yes; Does the text have a contrast ratio of at least 7:1 with the area of the background with the least contrast?
      • Yes; PASS
      • No; See F1.4.6.a.c Regular text over an image or gradient does not have at least a 7:1 contrast ratio with the background on the area of least contrast.
    • No; Is the text placeholder text?
      • Yes; Does the placeholder text have a contrast ratio of at least 7:1 with its background?
        • Yes; PASS
        • No; See F1.4.6.a.d Regular placeholder text does not have at least a 7:1 contrast ratio with the background.
      • No; Is the text a link?
        • Yes; Does the link text have a contrast ratio of at least 7:1 with its background?
          • Yes; Does the text in the hover and focus states have a contrast ratio of at least 7:1 with its background?
            • Yes; PASS
            • No; See F1.4.6.a.b Regular link text does not have at least a 7:1 contrast ratio with the background on hover or focus.
          • No; See F1.4.6.a.a Regular text does not have at least a 7:1 contrast ratio with the background.
        • No; Does the text have a contrast ratio of at least 7:1 with its background?
          • Yes; PASS
          • No; See F1.4.6.a.a Regular text does not have at least a 7:1 contrast ratio with the background.
  • No; PASS

Web Test Procedure

Desktop

  1. Use axe DevTools, WAVE, or ANDI to automatically check for text contrast issues.
  2. Use a tool such as one listed in the Testing Tools below to manually check any text not covered by the automated tool. For example:
    • Text in hover states
    • Meaningful text embedded in images (check the area with the least contrast)
    • Text over images or gradients
    • Text in modal and modeless dialogs
    • Text in menus
    • Placeholder text

    Note: Check hex codes rather than using an eyedropper tool whenever possible to ensure you're checking the exact color.

  3. Verify that all regular size text has a contrast ratio of at least 7:1 with its background.

Mobile

  1. Open the page on a desktop computer in Chrome.
  2. Right-click on the page and select Inspect or press Ctrl+Shift+I to open the Developer Tools.
  3. Activate the Toggle device toolbar button or press Ctrl+Shift+M.
  4. Select a mobile device from the Dimensions drop-down menu.
  5. Follow the procedure for Desktop Web.

Mobile App Test Procedure

iOS and Android

  1. Ensure that any contrast enhancements, such as Increase Contrast on iOS, are turned OFF.
  2. Visually check for text which appears to have poor contrast with its background.
  3. Take a screenshot on the device and open it on a desktop computer.
  4. Use a manual tool such as one listed in the Testing Tools below to check the contrast ratio of the colors in the screenshot.
  5. Verify that the contrast ratio between the text and its background is at least 7:1.
  6. If the app supports Dark Mode, test both Light and Dark Mode.
  7. If the app has any alternate themes that affect text and background colors, test each of these.

PDF Test Procedure

Use a manual tool such as one listed in the Testing Tools below to manually check any regular size text.

Failures

  • F1.4.6.a.a Regular text does not have at least a 7:1 contrast ratio with the background. (Medium)
  • F1.4.6.a.b Regular link text does not have at least a 7:1 contrast ratio with the background on hover or focus. (Medium)
  • F1.4.6.a.c Regular text over an image or gradient does not have at least a 7:1 contrast ratio with the background on the area of least contrast. (Medium)
  • F1.4.6.a.d Regular placeholder text does not have at least a 7:1 contrast ratio with the background. (Medium)
