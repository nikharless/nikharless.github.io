Basic Requirements and Best Practices

= Requirement
= Best Practice

  • Live video with audio must have synchronized captions.
User Story: As a deaf user, I want to read synchronized captions on a video so that I can get all of the same information that is conveyed through the audio.
Success: If you could not hear the video, when you read the captions, you would get all of the information conveyed in the audio part of the content.

Testing Logic

Does the page contain any live video broadcasts?

Note: This criterion does not apply to two-way multimedia calls between multiple individuals.

  • Yes; Does the video broadcast have a procedure in place to provide real-time captions?
    • Yes; Are the captions accurate?
      • Yes; Are the captions synchronized?
        • Yes; Do the captions include all dialogue?
          • Yes; Do the captions include important sound effects?
            • Yes; Do the captions identify the speakers?
              • Yes; Are the captions easy to read, or can their appearance be adjusted by the user?
                • Yes; PASS
                • No; See F1.2.4.a.g Existing live captions are difficult to read.
              • No; See F1.2.4.a.f Existing live captions do not identify speakers.
            • No; See F1.2.4.a.e Existing live captions are missing important sound effect descriptions.
          • No; See F1.2.4.a.d Existing live captions are missing dialogue.
        • No; See F1.2.4.a.c Existing live captions are not synchronized.
      • No; See F1.2.4.a.b Existing live captions are not accurate.
    • No; See F1.2.4.a.a Live synchronized media does not have captions.
  • No; PASS

Web Test Procedure

  1. Locate each live video element on the page.
  2. Verify that there is a procedure in place to provide real-time captions.
  3. Verify that the captions match the audio in both content and timing.
  4. Verify that the captions identify the speaker when it is not apparent.
  5. Verify that the captions can be easily read or that their appearance can be adjusted by the user.

Mobile App Test Procedure

  • Follow the procedure for Web.

Failures

  • F1.2.4.a.a Live synchronized media does not have captions. (High)
  • F1.2.4.a.b Existing live captions are not accurate. (Medium)
  • F1.2.4.a.c Existing live captions are not synchronized. (Medium)
  • F1.2.4.a.d Existing live captions are missing dialogue. (Medium)
  • F1.2.4.a.e Existing live captions are missing important sound effect descriptions. (Medium)
  • F1.2.4.a.f Existing live captions do not identify speakers. (Low)
  • F1.2.4.a.g Existing live captions are difficult to read. (Medium)
