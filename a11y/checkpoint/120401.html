Basic Requirements and Best Practices

= Requirement
= Best Practice

  • Live video with audio must have synchronized captions.
User Story: As a deaf user, I want to read synchronized captions on a video so that I can get all of the same information that is conveyed through the audio.
Success: If you could not hear the video, when you read the captions, you would get all of the information conveyed in the audio part of the content.

Web Test Procedure

  1. Locate each live video element on the page.
  2. Verify that there is a procedure in place to provide real-time captions.
  3. Verify that the captions match the audio in both content and timing.
  4. Verify that the captions identify the speaker when it is not apparent.
  5. Verify that the captions can be easily read or that their appearance can be adjusted by the user.

Mobile App Test Procedure
