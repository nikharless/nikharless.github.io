WCAG | 1.2.2 Captions (Prerecorded)

Prerecorded video with audio must have synchronized captions.

Success: You can read the captions without the audio and get all the information conveyed in the audio.

Basic Requirements and Best Practices

= Requirement
= Best Practice

  • Prerecorded video with audio must have synchronized captions.
  • For the benefit of deafblind users, a full text transcript with both audio and visual information should also be available.

Testing Logic

Does the page contain synchronized media (e.g., videos with dialogue)?

  • Yes; Does the video have captions for all the audio content?
    • Yes; Are the captions synchronized with the video?
      • Yes; If the audio includes more than one speaker, do the captions identify who is speaking each time it changes (if it is not apparent visually)?
        • Yes; Do the captions include all dialogue and important sound effects?
          • Yes; PASS
          • No; Include all dialogue and important music and sound effects in the captions.
        • No; Identify the speaker for all dialogue, if it is not apparent visually.
      • No; Synchronize the captions with the video content
    • No; Is the video itself a media alternative for text?
      • Yes; Is it clearly labeled as such?
        • Yes; PASS
        • No; Add text making it clear that the video is an alternative for the text content.
      • No; Provide synchronized captions which include all dialogue and important sound effects in the video.
  • No; PASS

Web Test Procedure

  1. Locate each video element on the page.
  2. Verify that it has captions for the audio portion.
  3. Verify that the captions match the audio in both content and timing.
  4. Verify that the captions identify the speaker when it is not apparent.
  5. Verify that the captions can be easily read or that their appearance can be adjusted by the user.

Mobile App Test Procedure

  • Follow the procedure for Web.

Failures

  • F1.2.2.a.a Synchronized media does not have captions. (High)
  • F1.2.2.a.b Existing captions are incorrect. (Medium)
  • F1.2.2.a.c Existing captions are not synchronized. (Medium)
  • F1.2.2.a.d Existing captions are missing dialogue. (Medium)
  • F1.2.2.a.e Existing captions are missing important sound effect descriptions. (Medium)
  • F1.2.2.a.f Existing captions do not identify speakers. (Low)
  • F1.2.2.a.g Existing captions are difficult to read. (Medium)
