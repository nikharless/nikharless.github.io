Expected Result

Content does not flash more than 3 times per second.

Actual Result

Content flashes more than 3 times per second.

Solution

  • Remove the flashing content, OR
  • Reduce the flashing to less than 3 flashes per second.

Examples

Why It's Important

Flashing content can cause migraines, dizziness, nausea, and seizures.

References

  • Understanding Success Criterion {{ sc.name }}
  • G19: Ensuring that no component of the content flashes more than three times in any 1-second period
