Expected Result

Elements are contained within any required parent elements.

Actual Result

An element requires a particular parent element, but the parent element is missing.

Solution

HTML

Ensure that any required parent elements are present.

  • <area> must be a child of <map>
  • <caption>, <colgroup>, <tbody>, <tfoot>, and <thead> must be a child of <table>
  • <col> must be a child of <colgroup>
  • <dd> and <dt> must be a child of <dl>
  • <figcaption> must be a child of <figure>
  • <legend> must be a child of <fieldset>
  • <li> must be a child of <ul>, <ol>, or <menu>
  • <optgroup> must be a child of <select>
  • <option> must be a child of <select>, <optgroup>, or <datalist>
  • <summary> must be a child of <details>
  • <td> and <th> must be a child of <tr>
  • <title> must be a child of <head>
  • <track> must be a child of <audio> or <video>

Examples

Types of paint

  • Watercolor
  • Acrylic
  • Oil
<p>Types of paint</p>
<ul>
    <li>Watercolor</li>
    <li>Acrylic</li>
    <li>Oil</li>
</ul>
