Expected Result

Elements are contained within any required parent element or role.

Actual Result

An element requires a particular parent element or role, but the parent is missing.

Solution

HTML

Ensure that any required parent elements are present.

  • <area> must be the descendent of <map>, but it doesn't have to be a direct child.
  • <caption> must be the first descendent of <table>.
  • <col> must be a child of <colgroup>.
  • <colgroup> must be a child of <table>. It must appear after any <caption> but before any <thead>, <tbody>, <tfoot>, or <tr>.
  • <dd> must be a child of <dl> (or a <div> that is a child of a <dl>).
  • <dt> must be a child of <dl> (or a <div> that is a child of a <dl>).
  • <figcaption> must be the first or last child of <figure>.
  • <head> must be the first child of <html>.
  • <legend> must be the first child of <fieldset> or a child of <optgroup>.
  • <li> must be a child of <ul>, <ol>, or <menu>.
  • <optgroup> must be a child of <select>.
  • <option> must be a child of <select>, <optgroup>, or <datalist>.
  • <summary> must be a child of <details>.
  • <tbody> must be a child of <table>. It must be after any <caption>, <colgroup>, or <thead>.
  • <td> must be a child of <tr>.
  • <tfoot> must be a child of <table>. It must appear after any <caption>, <colgroup>, <thead>, <tbody>, or <tr>.
  • <th> must be a child of <tr>.
  • <thead> must be a child of <table>. It must appear after any <caption> or <colgroup> but before any <tbody>, <tfoot>, or <tr>.
  • <title> must be a child of <head>. Only one <title> is permitted.
  • <tr> must be a child of <table> if the <table> has no <tbody>. In this case, it must appear after any <caption>, <colgroup>, or <thead>. Otherwise, it must be a child of <thead>, <tbody>, or <tfoot>.
  • <track> must be a child of <audio> or <video>.

ARIA

Ensure that any required parent roles are present.

  • caption must be a child of figure, grid, table, or treegrid.
  • cell must be a child of row.
  • columnheader must be a child of row.
  • gridcell must be a child of row.
  • listitem must be a child of directory or list.
  • menuitem must be a child of group, menu, or menubar.
  • menuitemcheckbox must be a child of group, menu, or menubar.
  • menuitemradio must be a child of group, menu, or menubar.
  • option must be a child of group or listbox.
  • row must be a child of grid, rowgroup, table, or treegrid.
  • rowgroup must be a child of grid, table, or treegrid.
  • rowheader must be a child of row.
  • tab must be a child of tablist.
  • treeitem must be a child of group or tree.

Failing Example

Passing Example

List

In this example, each <li> is a child of the <ul>.

Types of paint

  • Watercolor
  • Acrylic
  • Oil
<p>Types of paint</p>
    <ul>
        <li>Watercolor</li>
        <li>Acrylic</li>
        <li>Oil</li>
    </ul>

Not Applicable

The following items are not applicable to this failure:

  • Role of "none":
    <li role="none">
    Does not appear in accessibility tree

Disabilities Affected

  • Blindness/Vision Impairment: Programmatic roles enable compatibility with assistive technology such as screen readers and screen magnifiers.
  • Motor Impairment: Programmatic roles enable compatibility with assistive technology such as speech recognition software.
