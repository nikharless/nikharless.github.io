WCAG | 1.2.1 Audio-only and Video-only (Prerecorded) | 1.2.1.a Text Transcript (Prerecorded Audio-only)

Expected Result Prerecorded audio-only content must have a text transcript that includes all of the information presented in the audio.
Actual Result The prerecorded audio-only content has a text transcript, but it doesn't include all the dialogue.
Solution Update the text transcript to include all of the dialogue in the audio.
Example

Transcript

In this example from the This American Life podcast, all spoken dialogue is included in the transcript.

Ira Glass Which got her thinking. And when she got back to work, without much to do, she decided to make a poster of her own.

Jessica Lee Williamson I just wrote “cat found” above two pictures of possums that I had printed off the internet. One of the possums looks like he’s crouching, disturbed by an invasive camera lens. And then the other possum is hissing.
Why It's Important People who are deaf, hard of hearing, or have difficulty understanding audio information for any reason need to be have access to the same dialogue heard in the audio.
