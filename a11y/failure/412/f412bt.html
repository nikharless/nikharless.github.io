Expected Result

Elements are contained within any required parent role.

Actual Result

An element requires a particular parent role, but the parent role is missing.

Solution

Ensure that any required parent roles are present.
  • role="cell" must be a child of role="row"
  • role="gridcell" must be a child of role="row"
  • role="menuitem", role="menuitemcheckbox", and role="menuitemradio" must be a child of role="menu" or role="menubar"
  • role="option" must be a child of role="listbox"
  • role="radio" must be a child of role="radiogroup"
  • role="tab" must be a child of role="tablist"
  • role="treeitem" must be a child of role="tree"

Examples

ARIA

  • Profile
  • Preferences 
<ul id="myaccount" role="menu" aria-label="My Account">
    <li role="none"><a role="menuitem" href="#" tabindex="0">Profile</a></li>
    <li role="none"><a role="menuitem" href="#" tabindex="0">Preferences</a></li>
</ul>
