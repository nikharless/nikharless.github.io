WCAG | 1.2.2 Captions (Prerecorded) | 1.2.2.a Captions (Prerecorded)

Expected Result If the video itself is an alternative to text on the page, it must be clearly labeled as such.
Actual Result The video is an alternative to text on the page, but it is not clearly labeled as such.
Solution Clearly label the video as an alternative to text on the page.
Example
Why It's Important Media which is intended as an alternative to text on the page needs to be clearly labeled as such, so users can find it and so users who expect text alternatives know not to look for them.
