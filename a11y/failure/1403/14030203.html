Expected Result

Large size text has a contrast ratio of at least 3:1 with all parts of the background.

Actual Result

Large size text does not have a contrast ratio of at least 3:1 with all parts of the background.

Solution

Change the text or background so that the contrast ratio between the text and the background is at least 3:1 in all areas.

Example

Adding a background color behind text that's over an image can help ensure that the text maintains enough contrast when the background image or the flow of the text changes.

Why It's Important

Vision impaired and color blind users need sufficient contrast in order to be able to read text.
