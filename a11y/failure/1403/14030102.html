Expected Result

Regular size link text, including all states of the link, has a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 with the background.

Actual Result

Regular link text does not have a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 with the background on hover or focus.

Solution

Change the text or background color so that the contrast ratio between the text and the background is at least 4.5:1 in all states.

Passing Example

This link text has passing contrast with the background at 4.55:1 in the hover state.

Not Applicable

  • Large text:
    File under {{ site.data.1403.1403[14030202].name }}.
  • Visited link text

Why It's Important

  • Vision impaired: Users often have difficulty reading low contrast text.
  • Colorblind: Some color deficiencies lower the contrast in the user's vision.

References

  • Understanding SC {{ sc.name }}
  • G18: Ensuring that a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 exists between text (and images of text) and background behind the text
  • G148: Not specifying background color, not specifying text color, and not using technology features that change those defaults
  • G174: Providing a control with a sufficient contrast ratio that allows users to switch to a presentation that uses sufficient contrast
