Expected Result

Large size link text, including all states of the link, has a contrast ratio of at least 3:1 with the background.

Actual Result

Large link text does not have a contrast ratio of at least 3:1 with the background on hover or focus.

Solution

Change the text or background color so that the contrast ratio between the text and the background is at least 3:1 in all states.

Example

This link text has passing contrast with the background at 3:1 in the hover state.

Why It's Important

Vision impaired and color blind users need sufficient contrast in order to be able to read text.
