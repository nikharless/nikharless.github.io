Expected Result

Hover and focus states of large link text have a contrast ratio of at least 3:1 with the background.

Actual Result

Hover and focus states of large link text do not have a contrast ratio of at least 3:1 with the background.

Solution

Change the text or background color of the hover or focus state so that the contrast ratio between the text and the background is at least 3:1.

Disabilities Affected

  • Visual Impairment: Users often have difficulty reading low contrast text.
  • Color Blindness: Some color deficiencies lower the contrast in the user's vision.
