Expected Result

Regular text, including images of regular text, has a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 with the background.

Actual Result

Regular text does not have a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 with the background.

Solution

Change the text or background color so that the contrast ratio between the text and the background is at least 4.5:1.

Examples

This text has passing contrast with the background at 4.55:1.

This text has very good contrast with the background at 11.44:1.

Why It's Important

Vision impaired and color blind users need sufficient contrast in order to be able to read text.
