Expected Result

Regular sized synchronized media captions have at least a 4.5:1 contrast ratio with the background in all places.

Actual Result

Regular sized synchronized media captions do not have at least a 4.5:1 contrast ratio with the background in all places, AND the appearance of the text cannot be adjusted by the user.

Solution

  • Ensure regular sized captions have at least 4.5:1 contrast with the background at all times, OR
  • Use a video player which allows the user to adjust the appearance of the captions.

Passing Example

Caption Settings

Some video players allow the user to adjust the appearance of captions.

Not Applicable

  • Large text:
    File under {{ site.data.1403.1403[14030206].name }}.
  • Text over moving images:
    File under {{ site.data.1403.1403[14030105].name }}.

Why It's Important

  • Vision impaired: Users often have difficulty reading low contrast text.
  • Colorblind: Some color deficiencies lower the contrast in the user's vision.
