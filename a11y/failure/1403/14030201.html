Expected Result

Large text, including images of large text, has a contrast ratio of at least 3:1 with the background.

Actual Result

Large text does not have a contrast ratio of at least 3:1 with the background.

Solution

Change the text or background color so that the contrast ratio between the text and the background is at least 3:1.

Passing Examples

This text has passing contrast with the background at 3:1.

This text has passing contrast with the background at 3:1.

This text has very good contrast with the background at 8.5:1.

This text has very good contrast with the background at 8.5:1.

Not Applicable

  • Regular size text:
    File under {{ site.data.1403.1403[14030101].name }}.
  • Disabled form controls
  • Purely decorative text
  • Visited link text
  • Logo text
  • Text within an image that contains significant other visual content

Why It's Important

  • Vision impaired: Users often have difficulty reading low contrast text.
  • Colorblind: Some color deficiencies lower the contrast in the user's vision.
