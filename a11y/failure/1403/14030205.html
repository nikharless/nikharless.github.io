Expected Result

Large text over a moving background has at least a 3:1 contrast ratio with the background in all places.

Actual Result

Large text over a moving background does not have at least a 3:1 contrast ratio with the background in all places.

Solution

Ensure large text over a moving background has at least 3:1 contrast with the background at all times.

Passing Example

Adding a background color behind text that's over a moving image can help ensure that the text maintains enough contrast throughout the animation.

Why It's Important
