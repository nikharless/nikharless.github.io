Expected Result

Regular sized text over a moving background has at least a 4.5:1 contrast ratio with the background in all places.

Actual Result

Regular sized text over a moving background does not have at least a 4.5:1 contrast ratio with the background in all places, AND the appearance of the text cannot be adjusted by the user.

Solution

  • Ensure regular sized text over a moving background has at least 4.5:1 contrast with the background at all times, OR
  • Use a video player which allows the user to adjust the appearance of the captions.

Passing Example

Adding a background color behind text that's over a moving image can help ensure that the text maintains enough contrast throughout the animation.

Why It's Important

  • Vision impaired: Users often have difficulty reading low contrast text.
  • Colorblind: Some color deficiencies lower the contrast in the user's vision.
