Expected Result

Regular-sized text has a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 with all parts of the background.

Actual Result

Regular-sized text does not have a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 with all parts of the background.

Solution

Change the text or background so that the contrast ratio between the text and the background is at least 4.5:1 in all areas.

Keep in mind that text flows differently in different viewports, screen sizes, and zoom levels, so while it may pass on a large screen, it could fail on a smaller one.

Failing Example

This text fails the minimum contrast requirement with the background at about 1.4:1 on the area of least contrast.

Passing Example

Adding a background color behind text that's over an image can help ensure that the text maintains enough contrast when the background image or the flow of the text changes.

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua. Rhoncus aenean vel elit scelerisque mauris.

Disabilities Affected

  • Visual Impairment: Users often have difficulty reading low contrast text.
  • Color Blindness: Some color deficiencies lower the contrast in the user's vision.
