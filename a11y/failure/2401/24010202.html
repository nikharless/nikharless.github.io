Expected Result

<iframe title> attributes describe the <iframe>'s contents.

Actual Result

<iframe>s have title attributes, but they do not describe the <iframe>'s contents.

Solution

Set the title attribute to a unique value describing the <iframe>'s contents.

Examples

<iframe src="header.html" name="header-iframe" title="Header"></iframe>
<iframe src="footer.html" name="footer-iframe" title="Footer"></iframe>

Why It's Important

Screen reader users need to know what sections they can navigate to.
