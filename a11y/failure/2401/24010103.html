Expected Result

A skip link is visible to sighted keyboard users.

Actual Result

A skip link is provided, but it is not visible when focused with the keyboard.

Solution

Ensure that the skip link is at least visible when focused.

Examples

Why It's Important

Keyboard-only users who do not use screen readers need to know what they are focused on.
