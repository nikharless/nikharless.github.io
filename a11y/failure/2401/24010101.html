Expected Result

A method is provided to skip blocks of content repeated across pages.

Actual Result

No skip link, headings, or landmarks are available to skip content blocks.

Solution

  • Provide a skip link, OR
  • Use landmarks to semantically mark up page content, OR
  • Semantically mark up content with headings, OR
  • Use a collapsible menu to hide blocks of links

Examples

Why It's Important

Screen reader and keyboard-only users are not able to scroll past blocks of content or go directly to the main content.

References

  • Understanding Success Criterion {{ sc.name }}
  • G1: Adding a link at the top of each page that goes directly to the main content area
  • G123: Adding a link at the beginning of a block of repeated content to go to the end of the block
  • G124: Adding links at the top of the page to each area of the content
  • H69: Providing heading elements at the beginning of each section of content
  • ARIA11: Using ARIA landmarks to identify regions of a page
  • SCR28: Using an expandable and collapsible menu to bypass block of content
  • PDF9: Providing headings by marking content with heading tags in PDF documents
