Expected Result

The user is able to pause, stop, hide or control the frequency of automatically updating content.

Actual Result

Automatically advancing carousel has no means to pause, stop, hide, or control the frequency of the slide change.

Solution

  • Provide a mechanism to pause, stop, or hide the slide advancement, OR
    • Note: The mechanism must allow the user to interact with other content while it is paused. Therefore, pausing on focus does not meet this criterion.
  • Provide a mechanism to control the frequency of the slide advancement.

Examples

This carousel has a pause button that stops the auto-play and tab buttons to manually control the slide display.

{% include examples/carousel.html %}

Why It's Important

Some people with cognitive disabilities and attention deficits cannot concentrate with continual movement.
