WCAG | 1.2.6 Sign Language (Prerecorded) | 1.2.6.a Sign Language (Prerecorded)

Expected Result Prerecorded synchronized media has a sign language interpretation for the audio content.
Actual Result The prerecorded synchronized media does not have a sign language interpretation for the audio content.
Solution Provide a sign language interpretation that includes all key elements necessary to understand the audio content.
