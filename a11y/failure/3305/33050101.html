Expected Result

When labels are not sufficient to fully describe the functionality, context-sensitive help is available for text inputs.

Actual Result

Text input labels are not sufficient to fully describe the functionality, but no context-sensitive help is available.

Solution

Provide context-sensitive help to fully describe the functionality.

Examples

Why It's Important

People with cognitive or other disabilities can complete their tasks more easily.
