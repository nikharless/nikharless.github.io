Expected Result

Sections of content must have headings or titles.

Actual Result

Sections of content do not have headings.

Solution

Add headings to sections of content.

Examples

<!doctype html>
<html lang="en">
  <head>
    <title>Cooking techniques</title>
  </head>
  <body>
    <h1>Cooking techniques</h1>
    ... some text here ...
    <h2>Cooking with oil</h2>
    ... text of the section ...
    <h3>Saut√©eing</h3>
    ...
    <h3>Deep frying</h3>
    <h2>Cooking with butter</h2>
    ... text of the section ...
  </body>
</html>

Why It's Important

People can orient themselves, especially those with cognitive or visual disabilities.
