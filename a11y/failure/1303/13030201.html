Expected Result

Instructions do not rely on auditory characteristics such as beeps, chimes, or speech to be understood.

Actual Result

Instructions rely on auditory characteristics to be understood.

Solution

Add an additional non-auditory reference that can be accessed by users who cannot hear.

Failing Example

Press Continue when you hear the chime.

Passing Example

Press Continue when the button is enabled and a chime plays.

Not Applicable

  • Visual cues:
    File under {{ site.data.1303.1303[13030101].name }}.
  • Sounds not referenced by instructions

Disabilities Affected

People with hearing disabilities may not be able to understand or follow instructions if they rely on a sound cues.
  • Deaf/hearing impaired: Users may not be able to follow instructions if they rely on a auditory description of the content.
  • Cognitive Impairment: Users may benefit from additional text cues.

References

  • Understanding Success Criterion {{ sc.name }}
  • G96: Providing textual identification of items that otherwise rely only on sensory information to be understood
