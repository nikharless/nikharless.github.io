Expected Result

A warning is provided about the length of a time limit that could result in loss of data.

Actual Result

The site has a time limit for inactivity which results in loss of data, but there is no warning informing the user of the length of time they have.

Solution

Provide a warning notifying the user of how much time they have before the site times out from inactivity.

Examples

Form with Warning

For security purposes, you will be logged out after 30 minutes of inactivity.


Disabilities Affected

Users with various disabilities often need more time to complete activities.
