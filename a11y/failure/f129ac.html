WCAG | 1.2.9 Audio-only (Live) | 1.2.9.a Audio-only (Live)

Expected Result Live audio broadcasts must have a text equivalent.
Actual Result The live audio broadcast has a text equivalent, but all the dialogue is not included.
Solution Include all of the audio dialogue in the text equivalent.
Example
Why It's Important People who are deaf, hard of hearing, or have difficulty understanding audio information for any reason, need to be able to access the same information provided in the live audio broadcast.
