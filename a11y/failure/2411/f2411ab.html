Expected Result

Focus is not entirely hidden by user-opened content.

Actual Result

Focus is entirely hidden by user-opened content and cannot be dismissed.

Solution

Ensure focusable content is not entirely hidden by content that the user has opened. Some ways include:

  • Reflow content to make room for the inserted content.
  • Put the overlapping content into a modal which can be dismissed.
  • Remove overlapping content when focus is removed.
  • Open additional content only over empty space.

Examples

Why It's Important

People who can't use a mouse need to see what has keyboard focus.
