Expected Result

Prerecorded video-only content must have a text or audio alternative that includes all of the information presented in the video.

Actual Result

The prerecorded video-only content has an audio description or text transcript, but it doesn't include all of the information presented in the video.

Solution

Update the audio description or text transcript to include all of the information presented in the video.

Example

Silent Video and Alternate Video with Audio Description

Click here for audio description.

Why It's Important

Blind users need to get all the information conveyed through video-only content.
