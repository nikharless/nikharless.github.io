Expected Result

Prerecorded video-only content must have a text (transcript) or audio (audio descriptions) alternative that includes all of the information presented in the video..

Actual Result

The prerecorded video-only content has an audio description, but it doesn't include all of the information presented in the video.

Solution

Update the audio description to include all of the information presented in the video. Descriptions should be neutral, clear, and present tense.

Important visual information may include:

  • Actions
  • Facial expressions
  • Indication of dreams or memories
  • Passage of time
  • Scene changes
  • Text embedded in the video

Why It's Important

  • Blind/vision impaired: People who are blind or vision impaired need to be able to access the same information provided in the video.
  • Information processing: People who have the ability to see use alternatives to more easily process the visual information.
