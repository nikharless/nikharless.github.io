Expected Result

When the meaning of words depends on the pronunciation, the intended pronunciation is provided.

Actual Result

The meaning of a word depends on the pronunciation, which is not clear from the context, and the intended pronunciation is not provided.

Solution

Provide the pronunciation with a mechanism such as the following:

  • Pronunciation written immediately after the word
  • Link to the pronunciation
  • Glossary with pronunciations included in the entries

Examples

Pronunciation After Word

He is a bass (/beÉªs/).

Link

He is a bass.

Why It's Important

Some people, including those with cognitive disabilities, may not understand the meaning of content.
