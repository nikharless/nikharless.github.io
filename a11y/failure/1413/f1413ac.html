Expected Result

When additional content is triggered on pointer hover or on keyboard focus, that additional content remains visible until dismissed, hover or focus is removed, or its information is no longer valid.

Actual Result

When additional content is triggered on pointer hover or on keyboard focus, that additional content disappears automatically.

Solution

Code the popup to remain visible until the user dismisses it or its information is no longer valid.

Examples

The tooltip remains visible until it is explicitly dismissed.

Informational tooltip

Why It's Important

Users with disabilities may require more time to read the content, to change magnification, to move the pointer, or to bring the content into view.
