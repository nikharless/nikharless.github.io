Expected Result

The user is able to pause, stop, hide or control the frequency of automatically updating content.

Actual Result

Automatically updating content has no means to pause, stop, hide, or control the frequency of the updates.

Solution

  • Provide a mechanism to pause, stop, or hide the automatic updates, OR
    • Note: The mechanism must allow the user to interact with other content while it is paused. Therefore, pausing on focus does not meet this criterion.
  • Provide a mechanism to control the frequency of the automatic updates.

Examples

Why It's Important

Some people with cognitive disabilities and attention deficits cannot concentrate with continual movement.

References

  • Understanding Success Criterion {{ sc.name }}
  • G4: Allowing the content to be paused and restarted from where it was paused
  • G186: Using a control in the Web page that stops moving, blinking, or auto-updating content
