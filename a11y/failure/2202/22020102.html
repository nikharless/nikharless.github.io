Expected Result

The user is able to pause, stop, or hide blinking content that lasts more than 5 seconds.

Actual Result

Blinking content lasts more than 5 seconds, and there is no means to pause, stop, or hide it.

Solution

  • Provide a mechanism to pause, stop, or hide the blinking element, OR
    • Note: The mechanism must allow the user to interact with other content while it is paused. Therefore, pausing on focus does not meet this criterion.
  • Shorten the blinking to last 5 seconds or less.

Examples

3-Second Blink

I will blink for 3 seconds

Why It's Important

Some people with cognitive disabilities and attention deficits cannot concentrate with continual movement.
