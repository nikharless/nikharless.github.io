Expected Result

The user is able to pause, stop, or hide scrolling content that lasts more than 5 seconds.

Actual Result

Scrolling content lasts more than 5 seconds, and there is no means to pause, stop, or hide it.

Solution

  • Provide a mechanism to pause, stop, or hide the scrolling element, OR
    • Note: The mechanism must allow the user to interact with other content while it is paused. Therefore, pausing on focus does not meet this criterion.
  • Shorten the scrolling to last 5 seconds or less.

Examples

Scrolling Text with Checkbox Control


Scrollin', scrollin', scrollin'
Keep scrollin', scrollin', scrollin'
Until you are controllin'

Why It's Important

Some people with cognitive disabilities and attention deficits cannot concentrate with continual movement.
