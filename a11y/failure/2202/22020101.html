Expected Result

The user is able to pause, stop, or hide animated content that starts automatically and lasts more than 5 seconds.

Actual Result

Animated content starts automatically and lasts more than 5 seconds, and there is no means to pause, stop, or hide it.

Solution

  • Provide a mechanism to pause, stop, or hide the animation,
    • Note: The mechanism must allow the user to interact with other content while it is paused. Therefore, pausing on focus does not meet this requirement.
    OR
  • Shorten the animation to last 5 seconds or less.

Examples

CSS Animation


I will play/pause

Disabilities Affected

  • Attention deficits and cognitive disabilities: users may be distracted by continuous movement.
