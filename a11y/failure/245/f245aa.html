Expected Result

There are at least two ways to locate a web page that is not part of a process on the site.

Actual Result

There are fewer than two ways to locate a web page on the site.

Solution

Provide at least two of the following:

  • Menu
  • Table of contents
  • Site map
  • Search mechanism
  • List of links
  • Links within content

Examples

Why It's Important

Different users can more easily navigate content in different ways.
