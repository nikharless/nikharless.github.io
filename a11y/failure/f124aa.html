WCAG | 1.2.4 Captions (Live) | 1.2.4.a Captions (Live)

Expected Result Live video with audio must have synchronized captions.
Actual Result The live synchronized media content does not have synchronized captions.
Solution Provide a means (such as CART) to produce real-time captions that include all of the information presented in the audio.
Example

Live News Broadcast

This local news live broadcast includes captions.
Why It's Important People who are deaf, hard of hearing, or have difficulty understanding audio information for any reason, need to be able to access the same information provided in the audio portion of the broadcast.
