Expected Result

Abbreviations must have definitions provided.

Actual Result

Abbreviations do not have definitions provided.

Solution

Add a mechanism for providing definitions for abbreviations.

Examples

Expansion with <abbr>

The title attribute on the <abbr> tag shows a tooltip on hover which reveals the expansion.

In the Christopher Nolan v. Batman lawsuit, Nolan was sued by the city of Batman, Turkey, for using its name without permission.

In the Christopher Nolan <abbr title="versus">v.</abbr> Batman lawsuit, Nolan was sued by the city of Batman, Turkey, for using its name without permission.

Hume et al. (2019) argue that...

Hume <dfn id="etal"><abbr title="and others">et al.</abbr><dfn> (2019) argue that...

Link to Definition

CAPTCHA tests must have alternate versions that don't rely on a single sense.

Definition with First Use

The WCAG is an international standard for making web content accessible to users with disabilities. The WCAG 2.2 has 13 guidelines.

The <abbr title="Web Content and Accessibility Guidelines">WCAG</abbr> is an international standard for making web content accessible to users with disabilities. The WCAG 2.2 has 13 guidelines.
The WCAG (Web Content and Accessibility Guidelines) is an international standard for making web content accessible to users with disabilities. The WCAG 2.2 has 13 guidelines.

Why It's Important

Some people, including those with cognitive disabilities, may not understand the shortened form of words.
