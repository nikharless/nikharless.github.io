Expected Result

Regular size link text, including all states of the link, has a contrast ratio of at least 7:1 with the background.

Actual Result

Regular link text does not have a contrast ratio of at least 7:1 with the background on hover or focus.

Solution

Change the text or background color so that the contrast ratio between the text and the background is at least 7:1 in all states.

Example

This link text has passing contrast with the background at 7:1 in the hover state.

Why It's Important

Vision impaired and color blind users need sufficient contrast in order to be able to read text.

  • G174: Providing a control with a sufficient contrast ratio that allows users to switch to a presentation that uses sufficient contrast
