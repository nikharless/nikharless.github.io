Expected Result

A new window, tab, or dialog is not launched without user request.

Actual Result

A new window, tab, or dialog is launched without user request.

Solution

  • Launch new windows, tabs, or dialogs only on user request.

Examples

Why It's Important

Content that behaves predictably is especially important to people with disabilities.
