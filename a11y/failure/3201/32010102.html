Expected Result

When a control gains focus, a new window, tab, or dialog is not automatically launched.

Actual Result

When a control gains focus, a new window, tab, or dialog is automatically launched.

Solution

  • Launch new windows, tabs, or dialogs only on activation.

Examples

Why It's Important

Content that behaves predictably is especially important to people with disabilities.
