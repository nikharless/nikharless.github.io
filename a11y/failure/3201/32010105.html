Expected Result

When a control gains focus, a change of context does not occur.

Actual Result

When a control gains focus, a change of context occurs.

Solution

  • Do not initiate a change of context on focus.

Examples

Why It's Important

Content that behaves predictably is especially important to people with disabilities.
