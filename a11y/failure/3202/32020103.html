Expected Result

When a control state or value changes, there is not a significant change of content before the control, unless a warning is provided beforehand.

Actual Result

When a control state or value changes, there is a significant change of content before the control.

Solution

  • Only add new content after the control, OR
  • Notify assistive technology of the change, OR
  • Provide instructions warning that the change will occur.

Examples

Why It's Important

Content that behaves predictably is especially important to people with disabilities.
