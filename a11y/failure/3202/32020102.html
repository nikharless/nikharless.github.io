Expected Result

When a form element's state or value changes, a new window, tab, or dialog is not automatically launched unless a warning is provided beforehand.

Actual Result

When a form element's state or value changes, a new window, tab, or dialog is automatically launched without warning.

Solution

  • Launch new windows, tabs, or dialogs only on activation, OR
  • Provide instructions warning that the change will occur.

Why It's Important

  • Blind/vision impairment: Users who can't detect changes of context may become disoriented when they occur unexpectedly.
  • Cognitive impairment: Users who have difficulty interpreting visual cues may become disoriented when unexpected changes of context occur.
