Expected Result

When a control state or value changes, a new window, tab, or dialog is not automatically launched unless a warning is provided beforehand.

Actual Result

When a control state or value changes, a new window, tab, or dialog is automatically launched without warning.

Solution

  • Launch new windows, tabs, or dialogs only on activation, OR
  • Provide instructions warning that the change will occur.

Examples

Why It's Important

Content that behaves predictably is especially important to people with disabilities.
