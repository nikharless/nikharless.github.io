Expected Result

The user has the opportunity to confirm or undo user-controlled data edits or deletions.

Actual Result

User-controlled data is edited or deleted without the ability to confirm or recover it.

Solution

Provide at least one of the following:

  • The ability to confirm the edit or deletion before submitting, OR
  • The ability for the user to cancel the action for a period of time after it is submitted.

Examples

Why It's Important

People with disabilities may be more likely to make mistakes, or not notice them.
