Expected Result

The user has the opportunity to review or confirm text answers before final submission.

Actual Result

Test answers are submitted without the ability to review or confirm.

Solution

Provide at least one of the following:

  • The ability for the user to review their answers before submitting, OR
  • The ability for the user to confirm their answers before submitting.

Examples

Why It's Important

People with disabilities may be more likely to make mistakes, or not notice them.

References

  • Understanding Success Criterion {{ sc.name }}
  • G98: Providing the ability for the user to review and correct answers before submitting
  • G168: Requesting confirmation to continue with selected action
