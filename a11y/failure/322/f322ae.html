Expected Result

When a control state or value changes, a change of context does not occur, unless a warning is provided beforehand.

Actual Result

When a control state or value changes, a change of context occurs without warning.

Solution

  • Do not initiate a change of context on focus, OR
  • Provide instructions warning that the change will occur.

Examples

Why It's Important

Content that behaves predictably is especially important to people with disabilities.
