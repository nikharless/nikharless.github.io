Expected Result

Unusual words or phrases must have definitions provided.

Actual Result

Words or phrases used in an unusual or restricted way do not have definitions provided.

Solution

Add a mechanism for providing definitions for unusual words.

Examples

Link to Definition

Unusual words or phrases include jargon.

Definition with First Use

The WCAG (Web Content and Accessibility Guidelines) is an international standard for making web content accessible to users with disabilities. The WCAG 2.2 has 13 guidelines.

Why It's Important

More people, especially those with cognitive disabilities, can understand the meaning of content.
