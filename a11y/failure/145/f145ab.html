Expected Result

Real text is used to convey information rather than images of text whenever possible.

Actual Result

Pages of text in PDF are images.

Solution

Use OCR to generate actual text from an image.

Adobe Acrobat Pro

  1. Open the document in Adobe Acrobat Pro.
  2. Activate Scan & OCR.
  3. Under Recognize Text, activate In this file.
  4. Select All pages and activate the Recognize text button.
  5. Select Correct recognized text to review any text that Acrobat is not sure about. Depending on the quality of the file, the text may need significant editing.

Examples

Why It's Important

Pages of text cannot be interpreted by screen readers.
