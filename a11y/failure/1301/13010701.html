Expected Result

Blockquotes are marked up semantically as blockquotes.

Actual Result

A blockquote appears on the page, but it is not marked up semantically.

Solution

Use <blockquote> elements to mark up blockquotes.

Example

If a man does not keep pace with his companions, perhaps it is because he hears a different drummer. Let him step to the music which he hears, however measured or far away.

-- Henry David Thoreau

<blockquote cite="https://www.brainyquote.com/quotes/henry_david_thoreau_141463">
    If a man does not keep pace with his companions, perhaps it is because he hears a different drummer. Let him step to the music which he hears, however measured or far away.
    <p>-- Henry David Thoreau</p>
</blockquote>

Why It's Important

Elements which have a semantic function that is visible at a glance for sighted users must provide the same semantic information to assistive technology users.
