Expected Result

Table captions are programmatically associated with the table.

Actual Result

The table caption is not programmatically associated with the table.

Solution

  • Use the <caption> tag immediately inside the <table> tag to mark up the caption, OR
  • Use aria-labelledby to associate a caption provided outside of the table.

Passing Examples

Table with <caption>

National Parks
Park Top Feature
Denali National Park Denali - North America's highest peak
Great Smoky Mountains National Park Clingmans Dome 
<table>
    <caption>National Parks</caption>
    <tr>
        <th>Park</th>
        <th>Top Feature</th>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td>Denali National Park</td>
        <td>Denali - North America's highest peak</td>
    </tr>
    ...
</table>

Table with Associated Label

Park Top Feature
Denali National Park Denali - North America's highest peak
Great Smoky Mountains National Park Clingmans Dome
National Parks
<table aria-labelledby="tableLabel">
    <tr>
        <th>Park</th>
        <th>Top Feature</th>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td>Denali National Park</td>
        <td>Denali - North America's highest peak<&/td>
    </tr>
    ...
</table>
<div id="tableLabel">National Parks</div>

Not Applicable

  • Layout tables

Why It's Important

  • Blind/visually impaired: Screen reader users rely on programmatic markup to understand how content is arranged. Table relationships are visible at a glance for sighted users. Assistive technology users need to get this relationship information from the programmatic structure and to be able to easily navigate the data.
