Expected Result

Color is not used as the only visual means of indicating an action.

Actual Result

Color is used as the only visual means of indicating an action.

Solution

Provide an additional means of indicating an action in addition to color.

Examples

Link Behavior

In addition to the magenta color, links that open in a new window also have an icon to indicate this action.

  • Contact
  • Help
  • Site Map
  • Privacy Policy
  • Terms of Use

Why It's Important

People who are color blind or have low vision need an alternate means besides color of getting the information.
