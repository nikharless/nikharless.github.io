Expected Result

Color is not used as the only means of distinguishing a visual element.

Actual Result

Color is used as the only means of distinguishing a visual element.

Solution

Provide a visual means of conveying the information in addition to color.

Examples

Highlighted Fields

Why It's Important

People who are color blind or have low vision need an alternate means besides color of getting the information.
