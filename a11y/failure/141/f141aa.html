Expected Result

Color is not used as the only visual means of conveying information.

Actual Result

Color is used as the only visual means of conveying information.

Solution

Examples

Required Fields Are Red

In addition to the red highlighting, the labels also indicate the required status with text.



Why It's Important

People who are color blind or have low vision need an alternate means besides color of getting the information.
