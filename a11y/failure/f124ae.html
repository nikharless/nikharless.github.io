WCAG | 1.2.4 Captions (Live) | 1.2.4.a Captions (Live)

Expected Result Live video with audio must have synchronized captions which convey the same information provided in the audio.
Actual Result The live video has captions, but important sound effects are not included.
Solution Include all of the important sound effects in the captions.
Example
Why It's Important Users with hearing may receive meaningful information from sound effects (e.g., the sound of someone arriving via train). Deaf users and other users who use captions must be given all the same information that is given in audio form.
