Expected Result

When content is inserted or changed, the reading order remains logical.

Actual Result

When content is inserted or changed, the content reading order is not logical.

Solution

Ensure that the dynamic content makes sense both in the overall flow of the page and within the context of the current sequence.

Failing Example

Content Inserted Before Trigger

When the checkbox is checked, an additional field is displayed. The user must navigate backwards with the keyboard to get to it, and assistive technologies have no way of knowing it is there without going backwards.

Passing Example

Content Inserted After Trigger

When the checkbox is checked, an additional field is displayed directly after the trigger, so keyboard and assistive technology users may navigate to it next in the tab order.

Not Applicable

  • Keyboard focus order:
    File under {{ site.data.2403.2403[2403].name }}.

Why It's Important

  • Blind/visually impaired: Users of assistive technology need to get the information in a sequence that makes sense.
