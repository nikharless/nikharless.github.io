Expected Result

Line spacing is adjustable to at least 1.5.

Actual Result

Line spacing is less than 1.5 and is not adjustable.

Solution

  • Set the line-height to at least 1.5, OR
  • Provide a mechanism to change the line-height

Examples

Bad Example: Single Spaced

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. Suspendisse et ultrices enim. Pellentesque pulvinar massa orci, sed ultrices tellus hendrerit ac. Ut turpis dui, dapibus id augue id, fermentum consectetur velit. Pellentesque pharetra hendrerit erat, ac convallis est lacinia quis.
line-height: 1;

Good Example: Space and a Half (1.5)

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. Suspendisse et ultrices enim. Pellentesque pulvinar massa orci, sed ultrices tellus hendrerit ac. Ut turpis dui, dapibus id augue id, fermentum consectetur velit. Pellentesque pharetra hendrerit erat, ac convallis est lacinia quis.
line-height: 1.5;

Good Example: Double Spaced (2)

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. Suspendisse et ultrices enim. Pellentesque pulvinar massa orci, sed ultrices tellus hendrerit ac. Ut turpis dui, dapibus id augue id, fermentum consectetur velit. Pellentesque pharetra hendrerit erat, ac convallis est lacinia quis.
line-height: 2;

Disabilities Affected

Users with certain cognitive disabilities have difficulty reading blocks of text which are single spaced.
