Expected Result

The width of a block of text can be adjusted so that no more than 80 characters are displayed without the need for horizontal scrolling.

Actual Result

When a block of text is resized so that no more than 80 characters are displayed, horizontal scrolling is required.

Solution

  • Use relative width measurements such as ems or percentages, OR
  • Do not set width on text containers

Examples

Use browser zoom to resize text to 200%.

Ems

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. Suspendisse et ultrices enim. Pellentesque pulvinar massa orci, sed ultrices tellus hendrerit ac. Ut turpis dui, dapibus id augue id, fermentum consectetur velit. Pellentesque pharetra hendrerit erat, ac convallis est lacinia quis.

Percentages

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. Suspendisse et ultrices enim. Pellentesque pulvinar massa orci, sed ultrices tellus hendrerit ac. Ut turpis dui, dapibus id augue id, fermentum consectetur velit. Pellentesque pharetra hendrerit erat, ac convallis est lacinia quis.

Why It's Important

Users with certain cognitive disabilities are better able to track lines of text when they are shorter.
