WCAG | 1.1.1 Non-text Content | 1.1.1.h Audio or Video (Text Alternative)

Expected Result The text alternative refers to the location of the transcript.
Actual Result The video element has a text alternative and transcript, but the text alternative does not refer to the location of the transcript.
Solution Add a reference to the location of the transcript to the text alternative.
Example

YouTube <video>

<video width="320" height="240" controls aria-label="blue tit chirping">
    <source src="blue-tit.mp4" type="video/mp4">
    Your browser does not support the video tag.
</video>
