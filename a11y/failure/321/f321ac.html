Expected Result

When a control gains focus, there is not a significant change of content before the focus.

Actual Result

When a control gains focus, there is a significant change of content before the focus.

Solution

  • Add new content after the focus, OR
  • Notify assistive technology of the change.

Examples

Why It's Important

Content that behaves predictably is especially important to people with disabilities.
