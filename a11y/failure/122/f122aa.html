Expected Result

Prerecorded video with audio must have synchronized captions.

Actual Result

The prerecorded synchronized media content does not have synchronized captions.

Solution

Add captions that include all of the information presented in the audio.

Example

HTML video

The Appalachian Storyteller

<video width="320" height="240" controls>
    <source src="molly.mp4" type="video/mp4">
    <track src="molly.vtt" kind="captions" srclang="en" label="english_captions">
    Your browser does not support the video tag.
</video>

Why It's Important

People who are deaf, hard of hearing, or have difficulty understanding audio information for any reason, need to be able to access the same information provided in the audio portion of multimedia.
