Expected Result

Prerecorded video with audio must have synchronized captions which convey the same information provided in the audio.

Actual Result

The video has captions, but speakers are not identified.

Solution

Edit the captions to identify speakers in the audio.

Example

Video Identifying Speakers

Why It's Important

Users with hearing can distinguish speakers by voices. Deaf users and any other users who use captions must be given all the same information that is given in audio form.
