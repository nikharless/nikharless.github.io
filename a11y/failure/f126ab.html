WCAG | 1.2.6 Sign Language (Prerecorded) | 1.2.6.a Sign Language (Prerecorded)

Expected Result Prerecorded synchronized media must have an accurate sign language interpretation for the audio content.
Actual Result The prerecorded synchronized media has a sign language interpretation for the audio content, but it is not accurate.
Solution Provide an accurate sign language interpretation that includes all key elements necessary to understand the audio content.
Example

Fake Sign Language Interpreter
Why It's Important Some deaf users are more fluent in sign language than in reading spoken languages. These users need an accurate translation of the audio content.
