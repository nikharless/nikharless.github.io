Expected Result

Focus is correctly managed during interactions and content updates.

Actual Result

Focus is lost or incorrectly placed during interaction or content update.

Solution

Ensure focus is deliberately placed on the next logical element during interactions and content updates.

Examples

Why It's Important

Navigating a site or application with only the keyboard will make sense.
