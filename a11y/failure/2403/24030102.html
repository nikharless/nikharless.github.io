Expected Result

Focus is correctly managed during interactions and content updates.

Actual Result

Focus is lost or incorrectly placed during interaction or content update.

Solution

Ensure focus is deliberately placed on the next logical element during interactions and content updates.

Failing Example

Focus Reset

When a tab is selected (Tab to tab list, arrow to tab, then press Enter), focus is reset to the top of the page.

Braille Device
Switch Device

Passing Example

Focus Remains

In this example, the focus remains on the tab when selected.

Disabilities Affected

  • Blindness/Visual Impairment: Screen reader and screen magnifier users can become disoriented if tabbing moves the focus in an illogical order.
  • Cognitive impairments: Users can become disoriented if tab focus is in an illogical order.
  • Mobility impairments: Users may have a more difficult time navigating a page if tab focus is in an illogical order.
