Expected Result

Each video element must have a text alternative which summarizes its purpose.

Actual Result

The video element has a text alternative, but it does not adequately summarize the content of the video.

Solution

Change the text alternative to a summary of the content of the video.

Example

HTML <video>

A text alternative that is something like "video player" would not tell the user what the video content is.

<video width="320" height="240" controls aria-label="blue tit chirping">
    <source src="blue-tit.mp4" type="video/mp4">
    Your browser does not support the video tag.
</video>

Why It's Important

Blind and visually impaired users need to have a text alternative to embedded video which summarizes the content so they know what it is without having to play it.
