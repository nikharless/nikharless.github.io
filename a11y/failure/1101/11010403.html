Expected Result

Decorative images must not have a text alternative

Actual Result

The decorative image has a text alternative.

Solution

Remove the text alternative from the image using one of the following methods:
  • Remove any text from the alt value in the <img> tag.
  • Remove any title attribute from the <img> tag.
  • Remove any aria-label attribute from the image.
  • Remove any aria-labelledby attribute from the image.
  • Remove any <title> tag from the <svg>.

Example

JPG Image

One might be tempted to give this image a text alternative such as "smiling girl with blonde hair and blue eyes," but this information is not relevant to the content. The image has only been added for visual interest.

If your children need health coverage, they may be eligible for the Children's Health Insurance Program (CHIP).

CHIP provides low-cost health coverage to children in families that earn too much money to qualify for Medicaid. In some states, CHIP covers pregnant women. Each state offers CHIP coverage, and works closely with its state Medicaid program.

Why It's Important

Blind and visually impaired users want decorative or redundant images hidden so they do not get overwhelmed with unimportant screen reader chatter.
