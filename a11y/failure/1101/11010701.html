Expected Result

Text characters used to represent other characters have a text alternative describing their meaning.

Actual Result

Text characters used to represent other characters do not have a text alternative describing their meaning.

Solution

  1. Enclose the text in a <span> element and give it role="figure".
  2. Add an aria-label attribute to the <span> element and set it to a text alternative which describes its meaning.

Example

Text Look-Alikes

ϲоοk 
<span role="figure" aria-label="cook">&#x03F2;&#x043E;&#x03BF;&#x006B;</span>

Leetspeak

|\/|4R|< 15 /\ n00b 
<span role="figure" aria-label="Mark is a newbie.">
    |\/|4R|&lt; 15 /\ n00b
</span>

Why It's Important

Without a text alternative, blind and visually impaired users would hear the actual text characters rather than what the author intended them to mean.
