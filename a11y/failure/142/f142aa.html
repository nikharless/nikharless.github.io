Expected Result

Audio that plays automatically for more than 3 seconds has either a mechanism to pause or stop the audio or a mechanism to control the volume independently from the overall system volume.

Actual Result

Audio that plays automatically for more than 3 seconds does not have a mechanism to pause, stop, mute, or control the volume.

Solution

  • Don't start the audio automatically, OR
  • Ensure the audio doesn't play for more than 3 seconds, OR
  • Provide a mechanism to pause or stop the audio, OR
  • Provide a mechanism to control the volume of the audio independently of the system volume

Examples

Why It's Important

Screen reader users may have difficulty hearing the screen reader when other sounds are playing. People with attention disorders may have difficulty focusing on the content when audio is playing.
