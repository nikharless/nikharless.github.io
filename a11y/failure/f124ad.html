WCAG | 1.2.4 Captions (Live) | 1.2.4.a Captions (Live)

Expected Result Live video with audio must have synchronized captions which convey the same information provided in the audio.
Actual Result The live video has captions, but all the dialogue is not included.
Solution Include all of the dialogue in the audio.
Example
Why It's Important People who are deaf, hard of hearing, or have difficulty understanding audio information for any reason, need to be able to access the same information provided in the audio portion of live broadcasts.
