Expected Result

Live audio broadcasts must have a text equivalent.

Actual Result

The live audio broadcast has a text equivalent, but speakers are not identified.

Solution

Identify the speakers in the text equivalent.

Example

Why It's Important

Users with hearing may receive meaningful information from sound effects (e.g., the sound of someone arriving via train). Deaf users and other users who use a text equivalent must be given all the same information that is given in audio form.
