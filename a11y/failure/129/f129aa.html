Expected Result

Live audio broadcasts must have a text equivalent.

Actual Result

The live audio broadcast does not have a text equivalent.

Solution

Provide a means to produce text alternatives that include all of the information presented in the audio.

Example

Why It's Important

People who are deaf, hard of hearing, or have difficulty understanding audio information for any reason, need to be able to access the same information provided in the audio broadcast.
