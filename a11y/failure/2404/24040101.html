Expected Result

The purpose of the link can be determined from the link text along with its immediate context without having to leave the link.

Actual Result

The purpose of the link cannot be determined without leaving the link.

Solution

  • Change the link text to describe the purpose of the link, OR
  • Include text that describes the purpose of the link within the same sentence, paragraph, list item, or table cell, OR
  • Add an aria-label attribute with text describing the purpose of the link, OR
  • Add an aria-labelledby attribute pointing to text outside the link's immediate context.

Why It's Important

  • Blind/vision impaired: Users can determine the purpose of the link without having to explore around the page.
  • Motion impaired: Users can skip links they are not interested in without having to visit the links to see what the content is.
  • Cognitively impaired: Users can avoid confusion caused by having to navigate to and from links to see what the content is.
