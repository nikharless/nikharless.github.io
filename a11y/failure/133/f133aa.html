Expected Result

Instructions do not rely on visual characteristics such as shape, color, size, visual location, or orientation to be understood.

Actual Result

Instructions rely on visual characteristics to be understood.

Solution

Add an additional non-visual reference, such as a text label, that can be accessed by color blind and screen reader users.

Example

Shape

Users are instructed to “click the shield to participate.” These instructions rely solely on the user’s ability to perceive and identify the shape of the image.

One possible solution is to include the word “shield” in the text alternative. This will identify the shape for screen reader users, as well as for users who have images turned off.

<img src="shield.png" alt="COVID-19 Customer Protection Standards shield">

Why It's Important

People with vision disabilities may not be able to understand instructions if they rely on a visual description of the content.
