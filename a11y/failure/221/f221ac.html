Expected Result

The user receives a warning before the time limit expires.

Actual Result

The time limit expires without giving the user a warning.

Solution

  • Provide a warning before the time out expires.
  • If the time limit is not essential or required,
    • Extend the time limit to at least 20 hours, OR
    • Provide a means to turn off the time limit before it occurs, OR
    • Provide a means to extend the time limit with a simple action at least 10 times, and give the user at least 20 seconds to do so.

Examples

Why It's Important

Users with various disabilities are disoriented by sudden changes of context.
