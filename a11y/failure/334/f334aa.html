Expected Result

The user has the opportunity to review, confirm, and or undo legal or financial transactions.

Actual Result

A legal or financial transaction occurs without the ability to reverse, check, or confirm it.

Solution

Provide at least one of the following:

  • The ability for the user to review the information before submitting, OR
  • The ability to confirm the information before submitting, OR
  • The ability for the user to cancel the transaction for a period of time after it is submitted.

Examples

Why It's Important

People with disabilities may be more likely to make mistakes, or not notice them.
