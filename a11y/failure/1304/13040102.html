Expected Result

Content rotates to accommodate changes in device orientation.

Actual Result

Content is restricted to a specific orientation.

Solution

Remove any restrictions on orientation.

Example

Why It's Important

Users with dexterity impairments often have their devices mounted in a fixed position, such as on the arm of a wheelchair. In addition, users with low vision may find it easier to view zoomed content in landscape orientation.

References

  • Understanding SC 1.3.4 Orientation
  • F97: Failure due to locking the orientation to landscape or portrait view
  • F100: Failure of Success Criterion 1.3.4 due to showing a message asking to reorient device
  • G214: Using a control to allow access to content in different orientations which is otherwise restricted
