Expected Result

Mobile app screen has a title or main heading describing the content.

Actual Result

Mobile app screen does not have a title or main heading describing the content.

Solution

Add a descriptive title or main heading describing the screen content.

Examples

Disabilities Affected

Users can easily identify whether the information on the web page is relevant to their needs.
  • Blindness/Visual Impairment: Screen reader users can differentiate content when multiple web pages are open.
  • Motion impaired: Users who rely on audio when navigating between web pages can differentiate content.
  • Cognitive Impairment: Users can identify content by its title.
