Expected Result

Mobile app screen has a title or main heading describing the content.

Actual Result

Mobile app screen does not have a title or main heading describing the content.

Solution

Add a descriptive title or main heading describing the screen content.

Examples

Why It's Important

Screen reader users, as well as people with cognitive, memory, or reading disabilities benefit from the ability to identify content by its title.
