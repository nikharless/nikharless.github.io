Expected Result

Previously entered information is either auto-populated or available for selection.

Actual Result

Previously entered information is not auto-populated or available for selection.

Solution

Ensure the user doesn't have to re-type the same information, by:

  • Auto-populating previously entered or provided information, OR
  • Providing the information for the user to copy and paste.

Examples

Why It's Important

Some people with cognitive disabilities have difficulty remembering what they entered before.
