Expected Result

Foreground and background colors can be adjusted by the user to suit their needs.

Actual Result

Foreground and background colors cannot be adjusted by the user.

Solution

  • Do not prevent the user from being able to adjust the colors, OR
  • Do not specify foreground or background colors, OR
  • Provide a color selection tool at the top of the page which allows the user to change the colors

Examples

Why It's Important

Users with certain visual or cognitive disabilities may have difficulty reading text in certain color combinations.
