Expected Result

Blocks of text must not be justified.

Actual Result

Text is justified.

Solution

  • Align text to either the left or right side only, OR
  • Provide a mechanism to remove the justification

Examples

Bad Example

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. Suspendisse et ultrices enim. Pellentesque pulvinar massa orci, sed ultrices tellus hendrerit ac. Ut turpis dui, dapibus id augue id, fermentum consectetur velit. Pellentesque pharetra hendrerit erat, ac convallis est lacinia quis.

Good Example

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. Suspendisse et ultrices enim. Pellentesque pulvinar massa orci, sed ultrices tellus hendrerit ac. Ut turpis dui, dapibus id augue id, fermentum consectetur velit. Pellentesque pharetra hendrerit erat, ac convallis est lacinia quis.

Why It's Important

Users with certain cognitive disabilities have difficulty reading blocks of texts with uneven spacing between words.
