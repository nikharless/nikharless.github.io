Expected Result

Audio that is mostly speech has low or no background sound or the background sound can be turned off.

Actual Result

Audio has background sound that is too loud.

Solution

  • Mix the audio so that there is at least a 20 db difference between the foreground and background sound, OR
  • Record the audio to minimize background noise:
    • Use professional recording equipment
    • Use a dedicated microphone near the speaker
    • Avoid cross-talk (talking at the same time)
    OR
  • Provide the user the ability to turn the background sound off.

Not Applicable

  • Audio CAPTCHA
  • Singing or rapping

Disabilities Affected

  • Hearing Impairment: People who are hard of hearing often have difficulty separating speech from background sound.
  • Cognitive Impairment: People who have the ability to hear may also have difficulty separating speech from background sound.
