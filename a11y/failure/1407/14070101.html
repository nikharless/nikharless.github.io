Expected Result

Audio that is mostly speech has low or no background sound or the background sound can be turned off.

Actual Result

Audio has background sound that is too loud.

Solution

  • Mix the audio so that there is at least a 20 db difference between the foreground and background sound, OR
  • Provide the user the ability to turn the background sound off.

Examples

Good: Audio Example: Foreground is 20 decibels above the background (mp3)

Bad: Audio Example: Foreground is less than 20 decibels above the background (mp3)

Why It's Important

  • Hard of hearing: People who are hard of hearing often have difficulty separating speech from background sound.
  • Information processing: People who have the ability to hear may also have difficulty separating speech from background sound.
