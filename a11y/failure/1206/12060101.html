Expected Result

Prerecorded synchronized media has a sign language interpretation for the audio content.

Actual Result

The prerecorded synchronized media does not have a sign language interpretation for the audio content.

Solution

Provide a sign language interpretation that includes all key elements necessary to understand the audio content.

Why It's Important

Some deaf users are more fluent in sign language than in reading spoken languages.
