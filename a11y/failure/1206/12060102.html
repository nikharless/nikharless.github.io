Expected Result

Prerecorded synchronized media has an accurate sign language interpretation for the audio content.

Actual Result

The prerecorded synchronized media has a sign language interpretation for the audio content, but it is not accurate.

Solution

Provide an accurate sign language interpretation that includes all key elements necessary to understand the audio content.

Example

Fake Sign Language Interpreter

Why It's Important

Some deaf users are more fluent in sign language than in reading spoken languages. These users need an accurate translation of the audio content.
