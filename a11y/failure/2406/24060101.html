Expected Result

Headings describe the topic of the section they head.

Actual Result

Heading does not describe the topic of the content it heads.

Solution

Change the heading text to describe the topic of the content it heads.

Examples

Food assistance

Get nutritious food through SNAP (food stamps), D-SNAP, and WIC for women, infants, and children.

Unemployment benefits

Learn if you qualify for unemployment benefits and how to apply.

Why It's Important

Users, especially those with cognitive or visual disabilities, can orient themselves.
