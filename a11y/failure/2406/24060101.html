Expected Result

Headings describe the topics of their content.

Actual Result

Heading does not describe the topic of its content.

Solution

Change the heading text to describe the topic of its content.

Examples

Food assistance

Get nutritious food through SNAP (food stamps), D-SNAP, and WIC for women, infants, and children.

Unemployment benefits

Learn if you qualify for unemployment benefits and how to apply.

Not Applicable

The following are not applicable to this failure:

  • Existence of headings
  • Programmatic markup:
    File under {{ site.data.1301.1301[1301].name }}.

Why It's Important

  • Blind/visual impairments: Descriptive headings enable screen reader users to predict what each section contains, including in lists generated by screen readers.
  • Cognitive impairments: Descriptive headings help users understand what the content is about.
