Expected Result

Labels and instructions clearly and accurately describe the purpose of the associated form elements.

Actual Result

Label or instruction does not clearly or accurately describe the purpose of the associated form element.

Solution

Change the label or instruction to clearly and accurately describe the purpose of the form element.

Why It's Important

  • Blind/visual impairments: Descriptive labels and instructions enable screen reader users to understand what is expected when navigating forms.
  • Cognitive impairments: Descriptive labels and instructions help users know how to successfully complete the form.
  • Mobility impairments: Descriptive labels and instructions help users avoid mistakes and having to re-enter data in form elements.
