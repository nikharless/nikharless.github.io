Expected Result

Labels describe the purpose of the associated controls or inputs.

Actual Result

Label does not describe the purpose of the associated control or input.

Solution

Change the label text to describe the purpose of the control or input.

Examples

Why It's Important

Users, especially those with cognitive or visual disabilities, can orient themselves.
