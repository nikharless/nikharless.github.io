Expected Result

Form inputs have persistent visible labels.

Actual Result

Form inputs have visible labels which are not persistent.

Solution

Provide labels which remain visible when data is entered in the input.

Examples

Floating Labels

Why It's Important

Everyone, especially those with cognitive disabilities, will know how to respond.
