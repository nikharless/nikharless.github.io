Expected Result

Visual indicators of a slider's boundaries have a contrast ratio of at least 3:1 with the background.

Actual Result

Visual indicators of a slider's boundaries do not have a contrast ratio of at least 3:1 with the background.

Solution

Change the color of the boundary to have a contrast ratio of at least 3:1 with the background.

Failing Example

Passing Examples

Fill

The fill of the slider bar has a contrast ratio of at least 3:1 with the page background on both sides. In addition, the slider thumb has a contrast ratio of at least 3:1 with the slider fill.

Disabilities Affected

  • Color Blindness: Users need to be able to distinguish non-text user controls.
  • Visual Impairment: Users need to be able to distinguish non-text user controls.

References

  • Understanding Success Criterion {{ sc.name }}
  • G174: Providing a control with a sufficient contrast ratio that allows users to switch to a presentation that uses sufficient contrast
