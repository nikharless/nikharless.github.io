Expected Result

Visual indicators of a control's checked state must have at least 3:1 contrast with adjacent colors.

Actual Result

Visual indicators of a control's checked state do not have at least 3:1 contrast with adjacent colors.

Solution

Change the color of the checked state boundaries to have at least 3:1 contrast with adjacent colors.

Examples

Checked Checkbox

The checkbox background has at least a 3:1 contrast with the external background, and the checkmark has at least a 3:1 contrast with the checkbox background.

Check this box

Checked Radio Button

The radio button background has at least a 3:1 contrast with the external background, and the radio button dot has at least a 3:1 contrast with the radio button background.

Check this radio button

Why It's Important

Color blind and visually impaired users need to be able to distinguish the control in the checked state.
