Expected Result

Visual indicators of a control's invalid state must have at least 3:1 contrast with adjacent colors.

Actual Result

Visual indicators of a control's invalid state do not have at least 3:1 contrast with adjacent colors.

Solution

Change the color of the invalid state boundaries to have at least 3:1 contrast with adjacent colors.

Examples

Background

The invalid checkbox and label background has at least a 3:1 contrast with the external background and the checkbox background.

Check this box (required)

Border

The invalid text box border has at least a 3:1 contrast with the external background. The internal background is not required to distinguish the control, so it doesn't need to meet the contrast requirement.

First name is required.

Why It's Important

Color blind and visually impaired users need to be able to distinguish the control in the invalid state.
