Expected Result

Visual indicators of a text box's boundaries must have at least 3:1 contrast with the background.

Actual Result

Visual indicators of a text box's boundaries do not have at least 3:1 contrast with the background.

Solution

Change the color of the boundary to have at least 3:1 contrast with the background.

Examples

Border

The border has a contrast ratio of at least 3:1 with both the internal and external background.


Background

The background of the text field has a contrast ratio of at least 3:1 with the external background.


Background and Border

The background of the text field has a contrast ratio of at least 3:1 with the external background. The border isn't required to distinguish the text field, so it doesn't need to meet the contrast ratio.


Background and Underline

The underline has a contrast ratio of at least 3:1 with both the internal and external background. The background is not required to distinguish the text field, so it doesn't need to meet the contrast ratio.


Why It's Important

Color blind and visually impaired users need to be able to distinguish non-text user controls.
