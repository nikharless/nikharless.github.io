Expected Result

Visual indicators of a checkbox or radio button's boundaries must have at least 3:1 contrast with the background.

Actual Result

Visual indicators of a checkbox or radio button's boundaries do not have at least 3:1 contrast with the background.

Solution

Change the color of the boundary to have at least 3:1 contrast with the background.

Examples

Border

The border has a contrast ratio of at least 3:1 with both the internal and external background.

Check this radio button

Background

The background of the checkbox or radio button has a contrast ratio of at least 3:1 with the external background.

Check this box
Check this radio button

Background and Border

The background of the checkbox or radio button has a contrast ratio of at least 3:1 with the external background. The border isn't required to distinguish the control, so it doesn't need to meet the contrast ratio.

Check this box
Check this radio button

Why It's Important

Color blind and visually impaired users need to be able to distinguish non-text user controls.
