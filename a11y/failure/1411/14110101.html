Expected Result

Visual indicators of a checkbox or radio button's boundaries have a contrast ratio of at least 3:1 with the background.

Actual Result

Visual indicators of a checkbox or radio button's boundaries do not have a contrast ratio of at least 3:1 with the background.

Solution

Change the color of the boundary to have a contrast ratio of at least 3:1 with the background.

Failing Example

Passing Examples

Border

The border has a contrast ratio of at least 3:1 with both the fill and the page background.

Fill and Border

The fill of the checkbox or radio button has a contrast ratio of at least 3:1 with the background. The border isn't required to distinguish the control, so it doesn't need to meet the contrast ratio.
