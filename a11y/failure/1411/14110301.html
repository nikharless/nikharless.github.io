Expected Result

Single-color icon has 3:1 contrast with its background.

Actual Result

Single-color icon does not have 3:1 contrast with its background.

Solution

Change the color of the icon to have at least 3:1 contrast with its background.

Examples

Why It's Important

Color blind and visually impaired users need to be able to distinguish meaningful icons.
