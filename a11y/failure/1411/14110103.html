Expected Result

Visual indicators of a switch's boundaries must have at least 3:1 contrast with the background.

Actual Result

Visual indicators of a switch's boundaries do not have at least 3:1 contrast with the background.

Solution

Change the color of the boundary to have at least 3:1 contrast with the background.

Examples

Border

The borders of both the switch container and the switch thumb have a contrast ratio of at least 3:1 with both the internal and external backgrounds.

Background

The backgrounds of both the switch container and the switch thumb have a contrast ratio of at least 3:1 with their external backgrounds.

Why It's Important

Color blind and visually impaired users need to be able to distinguish non-text user controls.
