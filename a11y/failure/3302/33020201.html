Expected Result

Form provides visible essential instructions such as required format, special conditions, etc.

Actual Result

Form does not provide visible essential instructions.

Solution

  • Provide essential instructions within the visible labels, OR
  • Provide essential instructions in text elsewhere in the form.

Examples

Individual Labels

Form Instructions

Spouse

Why It's Important

  • Cognitive impairments: Users understand what information should be entered.
  • Vision impairments: Labels and instructions help prevent mistakes, which help prevent users having to navigate through the page multiple times.

References

  • General:
    • G89: Providing expected data format and example
    • G131: Providing descriptive labels
    • G184: Providing text instructions at the beginning of a form or set of fields that describes the necessary input
