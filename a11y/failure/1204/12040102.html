Expected Result

Live video with audio must have synchronized captions which convey the same information provided in the audio.

Actual Result

The live video has captions, but they are inaccurate.

Solution

If you are using automatic captions, switch to manually written ones.

Example

Why It's Important

People who are deaf, hard of hearing, or have difficulty understanding audio information for any reason, need to be able to access the same information provided in the audio portion of live broadcasts.
