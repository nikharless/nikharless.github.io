Expected Result

Live captions must be synchronized.

Actual Result

The live video has captions, but they are not synchronized.

Solution

Synchronize the captions with the audio.

Example

Why It's Important

People who are deaf or hard of hearing generally can also read lips. When the video and captions donâ€™t match, it can cause confusion.
  • Deaf/hard of hearing: People who are deaf or hard of hearing need to be able to access the same information provided in the audio portion of the broadcast.
  • Information processing: People who have the ability to hear use captions to more easily process the auditory information.
  • Non-native speakers: Captions help non-native speakers of the audio language understand the content.
  • Environment: Captions allow all users to access the auditory information in environments where sound can't be used or cannot be heard.
