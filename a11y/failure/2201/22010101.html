Expected Result

The user receives a warning before the session times out.

Actual Result

The session time limit expires without giving the user a warning.

Solution

  • Provide a warning before the session times out.
  • If the time limit is not essential or required,
    • Extend the time limit to at least 20 hours, OR
    • Provide a means to turn off the time limit before it occurs, OR
    • Provide a means to extend the time limit with a simple action at least 10 times, and give the user at least 20 seconds to do so.

Examples

  • A site logs the user out after a period of inactivity to protect their data. The site provides a dialog asking if the user needs more time. After 30 seconds, if there is no response, the user is logged out.

Why It's Important

Users with various disabilities are disoriented by sudden changes of context.
