Expected Result

Session time limits can be turned off, adjusted, or extended.

Actual Result

The session time limit gives a warning when it is about to expire. However, the session time limit is not essential or required and cannot be turned off, adjusted, or extended.

Solution

  • Extend the time limit to at least 20 hours, OR
  • Provide a means to turn off the time limit before it occurs, OR
  • Provide a means to extend the time limit with a simple action at least 10 times, and give the user at least 20 seconds to do so.

Examples

  • A site logs the user out after a period of inactivity to protect their data. The site provides a dialog asking if the user needs more time. The user can extend the time limit an indefinite number of times. After 30 seconds, if there is no response, the user is logged out.

Why It's Important

Users with various disabilities often need more time to complete activities.

References

  • G198: Providing a way for the user to turn the time limit off
