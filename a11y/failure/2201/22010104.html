Expected Result

Time limits can be turned off, adjusted, or extended.

Actual Result

The time limit gives a warning when it is about to expire. However, the time limit is not essential or required and cannot be turned off, adjusted, or extended.

Solution

  • Extend the time limit to at least 20 hours, OR
  • Provide a means to turn off the time limit before it occurs, OR
  • Provide a means to extend the time limit with a simple action at least 10 times, and give the user at least 20 seconds to do so.

Examples

  • A web page has automatically updating featured stories links which are displayed in a rotating fashion. It provides a control that allows the user to extend the length of time between each update as much as 10 times the default.
  • An animation includes text that appears and disappears. A pause button is provided so that users who have trouble reading the text before it disappears can read it.

Why It's Important

Users with various disabilities often need more time to complete activities.
