Expected Result

Prerecorded video with audio must have synchronized captions which convey the same information provided in the audio.

Actual Result

The video has captions, but they are inaccurate.

Solution

Rewrite the captions to match the audio. If you are using automatic captions, switch to manually written ones.

Example

YouTube

Why It's Important

People who are deaf, hard of hearing, or have difficulty understanding audio information for any reason, need to be able to access the same information provided in the audio portion of multimedia.
