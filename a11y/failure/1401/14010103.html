Expected Result

Color is not used as the only visual means of prompting a response.

Actual Result

Color is used as the only visual means of prompting a response.

Solution

Provide a visual means of conveying the information in addition to color.

Examples

Highlighted Fields

In addition to the yellow highlighting, instructions name the field.

Begin by entering the 6-digit ID number into the ID field and clicking Search.



Disabilities Affected

  • Blindness/Visual Impairment: Users cannot see colors or have limited color vision.
  • Colorblind: Users cannot distinguish between colors.
  • Cognitive Impairment: Users may benefit from additional text cues.

References

  • Understanding Success Criterion {{ sc.name }}
  • F81: Failure of Success Criterion 1.4.1 due to identifying required or error fields using color differences only
  • G14: Ensuring that information conveyed by color differences is also available in text
