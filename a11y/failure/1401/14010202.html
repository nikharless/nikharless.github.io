Expected Result

Color is not used as the only visual means of conveying additional meaning between links.

Actual Result

Color is the only visual means of conveying additional meaning between links.

Solution

Provide a visual means of conveying additional meaning between links.

Examples

Different Colored Links for Existing and Missing Target Pages

Wikis often use different colored links for these purposes.

Why It's Important

People who are color blind or have low vision need an alternate means besides color of telling the difference between different types of links.
