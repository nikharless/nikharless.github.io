Expected Result

Color is not used as the only visual means of indicating an action.

Actual Result

Color is used as the only visual means of indicating an action.

Solution

Provide a visual means of indicating an action in addition to color.

Examples

Link Behavior

In addition to the magenta color, links that open in a new window also have an icon with a text alternative to indicate this action.

  • Contact
  • Help
  • Site Map
  • Privacy Policy
  • Terms of Use

Status Updates

When a step in a process is completed, the step label turns green. However, there is only a 1.2:1 contrast ratio between the complete and incomplete states. In addition to the color change, text is also added to indicate the action.

  • Step 1: Complete
  • Step 2: Complete
  • Step 3
  • Step 4
  • Step 5

Why It's Important

People who are color blind or have low vision need an alternate means besides color of getting the information.

References
