Expected Result

Captions must be accurate.

Actual Result

The video has captions, but they are inaccurate.

Solution

Rewrite the captions to match the audio. If you are using automatic captions, switch to manually written ones.

Example

Bad Example: YouTube

This video shows an example of inaccurate captions generated automatically.

Why It's Important

  • Deaf/hard of hearing: People who are deaf or hard of hearing need to be able to access the same information provided in the audio portion of multimedia.
  • Information processing: People who have the ability to hear use captions to more easily process the auditory information.
  • Non-native speakers: Captions help non-native speakers of the audio language understand the content.
  • Environment: Captions allow all users to access the auditory information in environments where sound can't be used or cannot be heard.
  • SEO: Captions allow search engines to catalog the information contained in videos.
