Expected Result

Prerecorded video with audio must have synchronized captions which convey the same information provided in the audio.

Actual Result

The video has captions, but important sound effects are not included.

Solution

Edit the captions to include all of the important sound effects in the audio.

Example

Video With and Without Sound Descriptions

This video demonstrates why including sounds in the captions is important.

Why It's Important

Users with hearing may receive meaningful information from sound effects (e.g., the sound of someone arriving via train). Deaf users and other users who use captions must be given all the same information that is given in audio form.
