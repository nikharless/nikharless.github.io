WCAG | 1.2.4 Captions (Live) | 1.2.4.a Captions (Live)

Expected Result Live video with audio must have synchronized captions which convey the same information provided in the audio.
Actual Result The live video has captions, but they are not synchronized.
Solution Synchronize the captions with the audio.
Example
