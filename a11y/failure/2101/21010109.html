Expected Result

Keyboard functionality does not require specific timings for individual keystrokes.

Actual Result

Keyboard functionality requires specific timings for individual keystrokes.

Solution

  • Do not require specific timings for individual keystrokes, such as repeated keystrokes within a short period of time or holding a key down for an extended period in order to register the keystroke, OR
  • Provide a separate, equivalent keyboard accessible method which does not require specific timing.

Example

Why It's Important

  • Blind/vision impaired: Blind users cannot use devices such as mice which require eye-hand coordination.
  • Hand tremors: Users have difficulty using a mouse and rely on a keyboard.
