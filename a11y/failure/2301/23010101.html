Expected Result

Content does not flash more than 3 times per second, OR the flash is below the flash thresholds.

Actual Result

Content flashes more than 3 times per second and is above the flash thresholds.

Solution

  • Remove the flashing content, OR
  • Reduce the flashing to less than 3 flashes per second, OR
  • Ensure the flashing is below the flash thresholds.

Examples

Fewer than 3 Flashes per Second

Time is running out!

Why It's Important

  • Seizure disorders: Flashing content can cause migraines, dizziness, nausea, and seizures.

References

  • Understanding Success Criterion {{ sc.name }}
  • G15: Using a tool to ensure that content does not violate the general flash threshold or red flash threshold
  • G19: Ensuring that no component of the content flashes more than three times in any 1-second period
  • G176: Keeping the flashing area small enough
