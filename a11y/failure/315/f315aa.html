Expected Result

When text requires more than a 9th grade reading level to understand, supplemental content explaining the text or an alternate version that is easier to read is provided.

Actual Result

No supplemental content or alternate version is provided.

Solution

  • Provide supplemental content such as explanatory graphics, audio, etc., OR
  • Provide an alternate version of the text content that is easier to read.

Examples

Why It's Important

More people, especially those with cognitive disabilities, can understand the meaning of content.
