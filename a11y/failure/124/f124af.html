Expected Result

Live video with audio must have synchronized captions which convey the same information provided in the audio.

Actual Result

The live video has captions, but speakers are not identified.

Solution

Identify speakers in the audio.

Example

Why It's Important

Users with hearing can distinguish speakers by voices. Deaf users and any other users who use captions must be given all the same information that is given in audio form.
