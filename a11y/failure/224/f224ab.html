Expected Result

The user is able to postpone or suppress interruptions.

Actual Result

There are non-emergency notifications or alerts, and the user has no means to postpone or suppress them.

Solution

Provide a mechanism to turn off non-emergency notifications and alerts.

Examples

  • A website has preference settings where a user can opt to turn off non-emergency notifications and alerts.

Why It's Important

Users with attention deficits and users of assistive technology are distracted and disrupted by non-essential interruptions.
